Frequently Asked Questions

How does the PDF merger work?
Select or drag multiple PDF files, reorder them if needed, then click Merge. The tool uses the pdf-lib library to combine all pages into a single PDF â€” entirely in your browser.
Is my data safe?
Yes! All processing runs locally in your browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server.
Can I split a PDF into separate pages?
Yes! Switch to the Split tab, upload a PDF, enter a page range (e.g., 1-3, 5, 7-10), and download the extracted pages as a new PDF.
